"I wanted her with me — not just on a shelf."

When I lost my pup Roxy, I found myself searching for something more meaningful than a jar of ashes in a closet. Most options were beautiful but expensive, so I started making my own — small resin talismans I could carry in my pocket. One day I reached in and felt the little Snoopy head, and for a moment, she was right there with me. That tiny moment of comfort changed everything. FROX was born so no one has to navigate grief alone, or empty-handed.

— Made with love, in memory of Roxy

The Process

Simple. Gentle. Meaningful.

✉️

We Send First

After you place your order, we mail you a small collection kit — a pre-addressed envelope and a safe baggie for a small amount of ashes. No errand runs. No confusion.

📬

You Send Back

Drop the baggie in the pre-paid return envelope and pop it in any mailbox. And if you'd like — feel free to tuck in a photo of your pet. We'd truly love to see who we're making this for. Every piece is crafted with more love when we know the soul behind it.

🤲

We Craft Your Piece

Your chosen shape is handcrafted with your pet's ashes set inside the resin — in your chosen color, made with intention and love.

📦

It Comes Home

Your finished keepsake arrives carefully packaged, ready to carry in your pocket, wear as a pendant, or display in every room you want them to be.
